Mango Music

Mango Music is a social media platform that allows users to share music that they are currently listening to! Users can create an account, login to create new posts, and even comment on other users' posts. Music is universal, so my team and I decided to build something that allows them to express what they're feeling about a particular song.

Smackin'

Smackin' is a fullstack web application clone of Yelp. Users can create an account and login to write/edit/delete reviews for a business. They can also utilize a search bar/ filters to narrow down their search results.

Drunken Maze

Drunken Maze is a 2D maze game, where the user has to navigate the character out of the maze! Of course, there are a few obstacles throughout the game, such as limited visibility, "enhanced movement", and a health bar that is gradually decreasing.
