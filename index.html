About Me:

I've always had a knack for problem solving and have consistently collaborated well in teams, so naturally pursuing a career in the tech industry seemed like the right fit. In my previous work, I've always strived to deliver exceptional customer service, so I understand the importance of providing users with the best experience possible.

I have skills in Ruby, Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, React/Redux, PostgreSQL, the MERN stack framework, HTML and CSS. In the near future, I plan on expanding my knowledge and learning Python and other languages/frameworks.

On a more personal note, I'm extremely competitive and have always been in love with sports. I'm constantly finding ways to improve and get better at everything - including in the work environment. Sports is one of my passions and it would be a dream to combine it with a position in tech!

Mango Music

Mango Music is a social media platform that allows users to share music that they are currently listening to! Users can create an account, login to create new posts, and even comment on other users' posts. Music is universal, so my team and I decided to build something that allows them to express what they're feeling about a particular song.

Smackin'

Smackin' is a fullstack web application clone of Yelp. Users can create an account and login to write/edit/delete reviews for a business. They can also utilize a search bar/ filters to narrow down their search results.

Drunken Maze

Drunken Maze is a 2D maze game, where the user has to navigate the character out of the maze! Of course, there are a few obstacles throughout the game, such as limited visibility, "enhanced movement", and a health bar that is gradually decreasing.
